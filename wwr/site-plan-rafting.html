Overview

Purpose

To show multimedia content about our services, expose our promotions, describe our branch locations, talk about our identity and history as a company, allow to our previous clients to share their experiences and give our online visitors ways to contact our customer service team.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Young singles and young couples (between 25 and 35 years old) with good salaries and financial education, families without small children, and tourists.

What do they want? They want to live moments of adventure, forget their worries, leave their comfort zone, see beautiful landscapes, and discover new places.

How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? My target clients usually make these internet searches using their cell phones or laptops.
